## 📹 Video Production Plan

To capture the best recording and make your post stand out, follow these simple steps:

### 1. Pre-Recording Checklist
* **Populate Mock Data:** Ensure your database has at least 3-4 spots with high-quality images, some approved bookings, pending recommendations, and 1-2 published event blogs.
* **Theme & Zoom:** Set your browser zoom to 100% or 110% (so the text is readable on mobile devices). Use a clean, distraction-free browser profile without bookmarks showing.
* **Recording Tool:** Use [OBS Studio](https://obsproject.com/) (free, high-quality) or [Loom](https://www.loom.com/) to capture 1080p video at 60 FPS.

### 2. Recording Sequence (Step-by-Step)
1. **Scene 1 (Landing Page):** Hover over navigation links, scroll down to show the venues, click on one.
2. **Scene 2 (Booking):** Click a date on the calendar, select the "Full Day" session, fill out the booking form, and click **Submit**.
3. **Scene 3 (Faculty Approval):** Log in as a Faculty member, go to recommendations, and click **Recommend**.
4. **Scene 4 (Admin Panel):** Log in as Admin, show the analytics dashboard, go to approvals, and click **Approve**. Wait for the PDF receipt to generate.
5. **Scene 5 (Finished Event & Blog):** Show the approved event on the calendar and display a beautiful event blog with a photo gallery.

### 3. Editing Tips
* **Keep it Fast:** Cut out any loading states, login typing, or form filling. Speed up those clips to 2x or 3x so the video remains fast-paced.
* **Music:** Add a upbeat, corporate/tech background track at low volume (-20dB) so it doesn't overpower your voice.
* **Zoom-Ins:** Add digital zoom-ins on key fields (like the calendar, the "Approve" button, or the generated PDF) to direct the viewer's eye.
